Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your CHLL app account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to: support@chllglobal.com.kh
  2. Include your registered phone number or email address.
  3. Our support team will process your request within 7 days.

Data that will be deleted

  • User account information (name, phone number, email)
  • Delivery address
  • Order history associated with the account

Data retention

Some transaction records may be retained for legal and accounting purposes for up to 90 days before permanent deletion.
